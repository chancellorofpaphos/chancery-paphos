Be it enacted as follows:

A new body politic, the Barony of [NAME] by name, is hereby erected and established within the County of [COUNTY] within my Duchy of Paphos.

Said Barony of [Name] shall be governed, under my protection, by one Baron of [NAME] who shall use and enjoy all the powers, privileges and prerogatives which barons of my Duchy of Paphos have heretofore used and enjoyed within their respective counties.

The powers of the Baron of [NAME] shall be discharged by myself, my heirs and successors, until said title is granted by subsequent letters patent.
