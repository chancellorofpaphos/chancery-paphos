Be it enacted as follows:

A new body politic, the County of [NAME] by name, is hereby created within my Duchy of Paphos.

The title of Earl of [NAME] is and shall be held by myself, my heirs and successors, Dukes of Paphos, until such a time as I, my heirs and successors, shall appoint, give and grant said title to another in subsequent letters patent.

Said Earl of [NAME] shall use and enjoy all the powers, privileges and prerogatives which earls of my Duchy of Paphos have heretofore enjoyed within their respective counties.
